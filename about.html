About: Samantha Contreras

Hey! I'm Samantha, owner of this site. I'm a student, studying Software Engineering at the Universidad Autónoma de Chihuahua (Autonumus University of Chihuahua). I was born in Chihuahua, Chihuahua, Mexico. I've lived here my whole life, and also, I'm a lucid dreamer.

I've always seen myself more as a thoughtful person, so it's was no surprise that I fall in love with the idea of having my own dream world, built by me.

When I first heard about it, I was amazed. I never thought about it before, but I had no doubt that it was possible. Since then, it haven't scaped my mind, and it's my favorite subject, though it's not very common. So, every time I have the opportunity to tell people about, I do, and that's what this site is about.

I assume you already know what lucid dreaming is, so I hope you were as amazed as I was when I first read about it.

My best wishes to you and the beginning of your lucid dreaming journey.
